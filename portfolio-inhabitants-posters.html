Welcome to the Inhabitants Universe

I was tasked with art direction for a series of 10 total planet posters which could reside in the Inhabitants universe - one for each planet, and one for the Moon. These were designed as part of a leadup to an IP launch, and needed to be in a retro sort of "pasteboard" style.

I worked with the fantstic Riley O'Kane to come up with a series of posters which felt cohesive in style while still trying to feel unique to the trends and tone of each planet in accordance with Inhabitants lore. Featured here are a handful of my favorites.
